Available osmlint processors:

osmlint bridgeonnode

  Identifies invalid nodes with bridge=* tags and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint bridgeonnode --box="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 osm.mbtiles

osmlint filterdate

  Creates a new MBTiles file with tiles newer than given date.

  osmlint filterdate --box="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 osm.mbtiles

osmlint filterusers

  Creates a new MBTiles file with only tiles containing geometries by
  given users.

  osmlint filterusers --box="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 osm.mbtiles

osmlint missinghighwaysus

  Compares OpenStreetMap to US Tiger data and outputs difference as geojson
  files to stdout.

  osmlint missinghighwaysus --box="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 osm.mbtiles tiger.mbtiles

osmlint missinglayerbridges

  Identifies bridges with a missing layer tag and sends them to stdout.

  osmlint missinglayerbridges --box="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 osm.mbtiles tiger.mbtiles

osmlint untaggedways

  Identifies ways with no tags at all and sends them to `stdout`.

  osmlint untaggedways --box="[7.4, 43.7, 7.4, 43.7]" --zoom=15 osm.mbtiles
